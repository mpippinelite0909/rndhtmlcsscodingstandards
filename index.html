Disclaimer

The standards presented in this document is based on Google’s HTML & CSS style guide. We do not claim that the contents of this document were authored and created by the UE-CCSS Research and Development Unit, but some modifications are made in accordance to the unit’s coding preferences.

Who Should Read This Book?

Everybody working in the UE College of Computer Studies and Systems’ Research and Development unit. Those with basic knowledge in web development. This coding standards does not restrict even those who never had any experience working in web, this is a great tool for starting up as they won’t even have to worry on having their own set of standards which may result to different programming styles amongst the member of the unit.

Students and Professionals. The coding standards presented in this document could also serve as a reference for those who are studying in schools and universities as well as professionals, freelancers and self-taught individuals.
